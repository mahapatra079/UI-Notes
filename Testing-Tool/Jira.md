# Jira

Jira is a project management and bug tracking tool.

---

## 1) Who Uses Jira?

- Developers
- QA teams
- Product Managers
- Scrum Masters

To manage tasks, bugs, user stories, and sprints.

---

## 2) Why Jira is Used?

- Track bugs
- Manage Agile projects
- Sprint planning
- Assign tasks to team members
- Monitor project progress
